How It Works

From inquiry to invoice in minutes

A streamlined workflow designed for busy studio operators.

1
Client Discovers
Clients find your studio on the marketplace or via your direct booking link.
2
Session Booked
Client selects room, date, and time. AI suggests optimal duration.
3
Deposit Collected
Stripe collects the deposit automatically. No chasing payments.
4
Reminders Sent
Automated email & SMS reminders 24 hours before the session.
5
Session Complete
Balance charged, invoice sent, client added to your CRM automatically.
6
Analytics Updated
Revenue, utilization, and client stats update in real-time on your dashboard.
